Tru Jet

TruJet was a regional airline based in India that offered flights to various destinations in the country. The airline was founded in 2013 and was headquartered in Hyderabad. TruJet's fleet consisted of ATR 72-500 and ATR 72-600 aircraft, and it has a codeshare agreement with the national carrier Air India. TruJet was known for its affordable fares and convenient flight schedules, making it a popular choice for domestic travelers in India.The airline commenced operations in July 2015 and ceased all operations in February 2022.
